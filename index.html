How To Play

Requests arrive gradually and explain who, where and why.

Equipment stays on the trolley after use. Medication can help, be wrong, or cause reactions.

Staff may ask to talk. You can talk now, ask them to follow you, send them to the quiet room, or send them to the meeting room and find them later.

People now have deeper emotional situations, follow-up conversations, memories, staff friendships, debriefs, colleague checks, trust, and recurring storylines.

Staff can ask to talk, ask for a debrief, worry about a specific colleague, need reassurance, or ask you to check on someone with them.

New starters can arrive and shadow you or another experienced staff member. Staff have consistent relationships, personalities, hierarchy, confidence and support needs.
