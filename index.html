How To Play

Drag around the world area. The game speaks what you touch. Press Activate Selected Object.

Example: patient asks for water. You leave the room, go to supply room, open cup shelf, take cup, fill it with water, return, and give it to the patient.

People can follow you to quiet rooms, toilets, family rooms, meeting rooms, staff room, cafeteria and wards.

Every important sound or request is stored in the phone/request log.
